What is Ripple XRP ?

Ripple, launched in 2012, is designed for fast, low-cost international payments. Unlike most cryptocurrencies, XRP isn’t mined; it’s pre-mined with a total supply of 100 billion coins. Ripple is widely adopted by financial institutions for its efficiency in cross-border transactions.


Where to buy Ripple XRP ?

Ripple is available on exchanges such as Binance, Kraken, and Bitstamp, where you can purchase it using various payment methods. In India, platforms like WazirX support XRP trading. Once you’ve bought XRP, store it in a secure wallet like Ledger Nano X or Atomic Wallet.


How to buy Ripple XRP ?

To buy Ripple, sign up on exchanges like Binance, Kraken, or Bitstamp. Complete KYC, deposit money through cards, bank transfers, or other options, and purchase XRP. Transfer your XRP to a safe wallet like Ledger Nano X for protection.
