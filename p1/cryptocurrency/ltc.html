What is Litecoin ?

Litecoin, created in 2011 by Charlie Lee, is a cryptocurrency built for fast and low-cost payments. Known as "digital silver" to Bitcoin's gold, it processes transactions faster while using a similar decentralized blockchain model. With a limited supply of 84 million coins, Litecoin is a reliable alternative for everyday use.


Where to buy Litecoin ?

Litecoin is available on major exchanges like Binance, Kraken, and Coinbase. Payment methods include bank transfers, cards, or e-wallets. In India, you can use platforms like WazirX or ZebPay. After buying, move your LTC to a trusted wallet such as Exodus or Ledger Nano X for added security.


How to buy Litecoin ?

To buy Litecoin, register on an exchange like Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken. Verify your identity, deposit funds using bank transfers, cards, or other payment methods, and purchase LTC. Move your Litecoin to a reliable wallet like Exodus or Ledger Nano X for added security.
