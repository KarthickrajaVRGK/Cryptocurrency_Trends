What is Uniswap ?

Uniswap is a decentralized exchange launched in 2018, allowing users to trade cryptocurrencies without intermediaries. Its native token, UNI, is used for governance, letting holders vote on the platform's development. Uniswap is a cornerstone of decentralized finance.


Where to buy Uniswap ?

Uniswap tokens can be purchased on exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, and Kraken. Payment options include credit/debit cards and bank transfers. In India, platforms like WazirX also offer UNI. Store your tokens in a secure wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet after buying.


How to buy Uniswap ?

To buy Uniswap, register on exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, or Kraken. Verify your identity, deposit funds using your preferred method, and purchase UNI. Move your tokens to a secure wallet like MetaMask or Ledger Nano X.
