What is Polkadot ?

Polkadot, created by Gavin Wood in 2020, connects multiple blockchains into a unified network. DOT, its native token, powers governance, staking, and interoperability across different blockchains. It aims to build a decentralized internet where blockchains communicate seamlessly.


Where to buy Polkadot ?

You can buy Polkadot on exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, and Kraken. Use payment methods such as bank transfers or cards. Localized platforms like WazirX also offer DOT. After buying, transfer your tokens to a wallet like Polkadot.js Wallet or Ledger for security.


How to buy Polkadot ?

To buy Polkadot, register on platforms like Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken. Verify your account, deposit funds, and purchase DOT. Afterward, move your tokens to a secure wallet like Polkadot.js Wallet or Ledger for safekeeping.
