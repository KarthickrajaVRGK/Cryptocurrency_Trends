What is Tron ?

TRON, created by Justin Sun in 2017, focuses on decentralizing the internet. It’s known for hosting decentralized applications (dApps) and supporting content creators. TRX, its native token, powers the network’s operations and rewards users for participation.


Where to buy Tron ?

TRON is available on exchanges such as Binance, KuCoin, and Kraken, where you can buy it using cards, bank transfers, or local payment methods. In India, platforms like WazirX also support TRX. Store your TRX in a wallet like TronLink or Ledger Nano X for security.


How to buy Tron ?

To buy TRON, sign up on exchanges like Binance, Kraken, or KuCoin. Complete the KYC process, deposit funds, and buy TRX. Transfer your TRX to a safe wallet like TronLink or Ledger Nano X.
