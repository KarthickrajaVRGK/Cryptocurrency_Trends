What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a digital currency that started in 2009, created by someone known as Satoshi Nakamoto. Unlike regular money, it doesn’t rely on banks or governments. Instead, it’s powered by a global network of computers. With only 21 million coins ever available, Bitcoin is seen as a unique and valuable alternative to traditional currency.


Where to buy Bitcoin ?

You can buy Bitcoin on cryptocurrency exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken, which allow you to trade using bank transfers, credit/debit cards, or local payment methods. For a more localized option, platforms like WazirX are popular in India.


How to buy Bitcoin ?

To buy Bitcoin, sign up on a cryptocurrency exchange like Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken. Complete KYC verification, deposit funds via bank transfer, card, or other payment methods, and purchase Bitcoin. Once bought, transfer it to a secure wallet for safekeeping.
